# eRepublik Scripts

A collection of userscripts for eRepublik to enhance your gaming experience.

## Installation

To use these scripts, you need a userscript manager extension for your browser:
- **Chrome/Edge/Brave**: [Tampermonkey](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/tampermonkey/dhdgffkkebhmkfjojejmpbldmpobfkfo) or [Violentmonkey](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/violentmonkey/jinjaccalgkegednnccohejagpfpbnig)
- **Firefox**: [Tampermonkey](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/tampermonkey/) or [Violentmonkey](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/violentmonkey/)
- **Safari**: [Tampermonkey](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/tampermonkey/id1482490089)

Once you have the extension installed, click on the script name below to install it.

## Scripts

### [Anniversary Quest Helper](https://curlybear.eu/erep/anniversaryhelper.user.js)
Displays total remaining time for locked locations and required miles and tokens for unclaimed locations for the Anniversary Quest.

### [BattleTracker](https://curlybear.eu/erep/battletracker.user.js)
Track battles from anywhere. Features include:
- Drag and drop interface
- Medal tracking
- Region tracking
- Battle timer

### [CleanProfiles](https://curlybear.eu/erep/CleanProfiles.user.js)
Cleans up user profiles by hiding unnecessary elements and focusing on important information.

### Company Manager (WIP)
Streamlines company management by adding a UI to the companies header. Allows for easy selection of companies based on industry and quality.

### [eRepublik Article Banner](https://curlybear.eu/erep/ereparticlebanner.user.js)
Displays the titles of the latest 10 articles in a scrolling banner at the top of the page. Refreshes every minute.

### [ErepCO](https://curlybear.eu/erep/erepco.user.js)
Adds a Combat Orders widget to the homepage, displaying current COs with details like reward, budget, and wall percentage.

### [eRepublik Laws](https://curlybear.eu/erep/ereplaws.user.js)
Displays the titles of the latest 5 laws on the homepage.

### [eRepublik Productivity+](https://curlybear.eu/erep/erepublikproductivity+.user.js)
Displays detailed productivity data for regions and country economy pages, including raw material productivity and quality multipliers.

### [eRepublik Feed Filter](https://curlybear.eu/erep/feedfilter.user.js)
Allows you to hide autoposts and mute specific users in your friends feed. Adds a "Mute for 24h" button to posts and a settings menu.

### [eRepublik Inventory Overview](https://curlybear.eu/erep/inventoryoverview.user.js)
Displays a customizable inventory overview. Features include:
- Toggleable categories (currencies, boosters, raw materials, etc.)
- Dark/Light theme
- Left/Right anchoring
- Storage usage display

### [TP Tracking](https://curlybear.eu/erep/tptracking.user.js)
Tracks your True Patriot profit. Displays a table with daily TP damage and CC earned, taking into account your country's tax rate.

### [WarsPage+](https://curlybear.eu/erep/warspage+.user.js)
A comprehensive overhaul of the wars page. Features include:
- Countdown timers for battles
- Battle sorting
- "Track All" button for BattleTracker integration
- Mercenary medal progress
- Division damage tooltips
- Epic battle visuals
- Free division detection

### [Zone Switcher](https://curlybear.eu/erep/zoneswitcher.user.js)
Enhances the battlefield page with a zone switcher.
- Displays damage, influence, and status for all divisions
- Highlights your current division
- Shows influence difference
- Dark/Light theme support
